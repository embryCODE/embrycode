About Me

Mason Embry is a graduate of the University of Louisville and has been a professional musician and music producer for over 20 years. Now based in Nashville, TN, he heads his own jazz trio, plays keyboards for Chris Young, and produces demos and master projects.

Embry has received training as a Full Stack Web Developer gaining proficiency in JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, AngularJS, and related web technologies. Whether producing music projects or coding for the web, he is a conscientious and detail-oriented hard worker. His greatest rewards come from finding creative solutions to difficult problems.
